About Me


Hi, and thank you for visiting my page!

Growing up I loved to draw and play video games. It was a form of expression for me as I didn't have much control in other aspects of my life. I went to a private school all of elementary and then spent four years in an even stricter high school: Hawthorne Math and Science Academy (yes, it's as terrible as it sounds).

While attending I was introduced to programming via Scratch, however, it wasn't until college that I seriously considered programming as a career. On a whim, I took C and Swift classes at my local community college. I hit the jackpot. I wanted to learn more. I needed to know more. The desire to create something I was proud of from the bottom up fueled my fire. It was then I decided to pursue a career in development.

I took the dive and enrolled in a Full Stack Development bootcamp where I honed my skills and watched hundreds of hours of videos on everything from basic Javascript to advanced algorithms.

Now, I look forward to diving deeper and expanding my knowledge in a world that is full of new, emerging and ever-changing technologies.

Proficiencies: Javascript, HTML/CSS, Node.js, MySQL, Firebase, React, MongoDB, Handlebars.js, Express, jQuery, Passport, JSON web tokens, Heroku, Material UI
